### Step 1: Visit Unsplash
1. Open your browser and go to [https://unsplash.com](https://unsplash.com)

### Step 2: Search for Dental Images
1. In the search bar at the top, type "dental clinic"
2. Press Enter or click the search icon
3. You'll see a grid of dental-related images

### Step 3: Filter Results
1. Look for images that show:
   - Modern, clean dental offices
   - Dentists treating patients
   - Professional equipment
   - Happy patients
2. Avoid images that look:
   - Overly staged or artificial
   - Low quality or pixelated
   - Unprofessional or messy

### Step 4: Select an Image
1. Click on an image you like
2. You'll go to the image detail page
3. Check the image details:
   - Resolution (should be 1920px or higher)
   - Photographer credit
   - License information (should say "Free to Use")

### Step 5: Download the Image
1. Click the green "Download free" button
2. Choose "Large" size (1920px) for best quality
3. The image will download to your computer

### Step 6: Optimize the Image
1. Go to [https://tinyjpg.com](https://tinyjpg.com)
2. Click "Choose Files" and select your downloaded image
3. Wait for optimization to complete
4. Click "Download" to get the compressed version

### Step 7: Rename and Organize
1. Rename the file descriptively:
   - Good: `clinic-interior-1.jpg`
   - Bad: `image1.jpg`
2. Place in the correct folder:
   - Clinic images: `/public/images/clinic/`
   - Team photos: `/public/images/team/`
   - Treatment images: `/public/images/treatments/`
